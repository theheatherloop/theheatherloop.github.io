About Me

I took the long way into tech, and that is my advantage.

I spent eight years in the food industry, five in business administration, and three in industrial automation before I finally admitted that what I enjoyed most was solving technical problems. For the last three years I have been deeply focused on software development, using nights and weekends to learn modern web technologies, databases, and automation.

I’ve already worked inside systems that were chaotic, outdated, or poorly documented, and I learned to untangle them. I can identify the real constraints, strip away noise, and build solutions that actually function for the people who depend on them.
